T1 Faker

Faker, whose real name is Lee Sang-hyeok, is a legendary professional League of Legends player from South Korea.

He is widely considered one of the best players in the history of the game, known for his exceptional skill, strategic thinking, and

versatility in playing different champions. Faker has been a key player for T1 and has won multiple World Championships.
